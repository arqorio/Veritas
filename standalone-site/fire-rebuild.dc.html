Good To Know

A few common questions

How do I claim the rebuild support? Just mention your project is a fire rebuild when you reach out — there are no documentation hoops to jump through.
Is the first consultation really free? Yes. The first consultation is free and comes with no obligation to continue. The 25% discount applies to work you choose to move forward with afterward.
Are the renders and blueprints ready for permit? Not yet — the renders and blueprints from the consultation are preliminary and for planning. When you're ready, we prepare full permit- and construction-ready sets as a next step.
What if my original plans were destroyed? That's common after a fire. We can start from photos, county records, or field measurements and redraw what was lost.
